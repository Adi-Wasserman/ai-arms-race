WHY THIS DATA IS HARD — Satellite tracking inherently undercounts labs that distribute compute across existing cloud infrastructure. xAI Colossus, Meta Prometheus, and OpenAI Stargate are giant, visible campuses — easy to photograph and measure. But Anthropic runs a three-cloud strategy across AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure, and Google's Gemini TPU fleet is spread across dozens of existing data centers. From orbit, these look like regular cloud DCs. Epoch can only track facilities with visible new AI-specific construction, which means their dataset structurally favors concentrated builders and undercounts distributed ones. Anthropic is the most severely undercounted — Epoch's satellite data captures roughly one-third or less of Anthropic's actual compute footprint.

SATELLITE-VERIFIED — What Epoch AI can directly observe: ~26 facilities with dated satellite observations, construction permits, and public disclosures. This is the most reliable view, but it is incomplete by design — it only shows what's visible from space.

+ CLOUD-LEASE ADJ. ⚠ — Adds sourced cloud-lease capacity that satellites cannot see. Three Anthropic legs plus Google's internal TPU fleet:
  • EAI-AWS — Project Rainier: ~500K Trainium2 chips live, scaling to >1M (Source: Amazon, Oct 2025). H100e conversion: Trn2 ≈ 0.93×H100.
  • EAI-GCP — Up to 1M TPUs, "well over a gigawatt" in 2026 (Source: Anthropic, Oct 2025). H100e conversion: blended ~1.4× (v6e + Ironwood mix).
  • EAI-AZR — $30B Azure commitment, up to 1GW with NVIDIA Grace Blackwell + Vera Rubin (Source: NVIDIA, Nov 2025). H100e conversion: GB200 ≈ 2.5×H100.
  • EGC — Google's internal TPU fleet for Gemini, not visible in Epoch's new-construction filter. Based on SemiAnalysis reporting ("millions of liquid-cooled TPUs, >1GW") and Fubon supply-chain analysis (~3.1M TPU production in 2026). Only the estimated Gemini-dedicated fraction is included.
All cloud-lease numbers are "up to" commitments — actual deployed capacity may lag announcements by 6-12 months.

TOTAL CAPACITY — Full compute at each facility as estimated by Epoch. For cloud provider DCs (Azure, AWS, Google Cloud), this includes all capacity — not just what's used for frontier AI training. Epoch flags confidence: CONFIDENT LIKELY SPECULATIVE

EST. DEDICATED ⚠ — Allocation bands estimating what fraction is dedicated to frontier AI. Tier 1 (85-100%): purpose-built sites. Tier 2 (25-40%): cloud partnerships. Tier 3 (20-35%): general hyperscaler DCs. These are assumptions, not observed data. The shaded band shows the low-high range.
